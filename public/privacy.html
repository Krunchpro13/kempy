Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

This policy explains what KEMPY ("we") collects, why, and your choices. We aim to collect only what's needed to run the Service.

1. Information we collect

  • Account: your email, name, and a securely hashed password (we never store your password in plain text).
  • Usage: your product searches and watchlist items, and preferences such as display currency.
  • Connected eBay store: if you connect eBay, we store your eBay username and an access/refresh token (the refresh token is encrypted at rest) so we can sync your listings, orders and profit on your behalf.
  • Billing: handled by Stripe. We store your Stripe customer/subscription identifiers and status, not your full card details.

2. How we use it

To provide and secure the Service: authenticate you, run research, sync your store, process subscriptions, and send essential emails (verification, password reset, receipts).

3. Third-party services

We share the minimum necessary with providers that power the Service:

  • eBay — to read/manage your store (only when you connect it).
  • Amazon product data (via Keepa) — to find comparison products.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — product titles are sent to match Amazon and eBay items.
  • Stripe — payments and subscription management.
  • Email delivery (Resend) — to send transactional emails.
  • Hosting/infrastructure — our database, cache and app hosting providers.

4. Cookies

We use a single first-party session cookie to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

5. Data retention & deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from Settings → Danger zone. Deleting your account removes your user record and all associated data — sessions, watchlist, connected-store tokens, seller preferences and listings — via cascading deletion.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed, one-time codes are stored hashed, eBay refresh tokens are encrypted (AES-256-GCM), traffic is served over HTTPS, and API responses aren't cached by your browser.

7. Your rights

You can access and update your profile in Settings, export your data via CSV where available, and delete your account. For other requests, contact us.

8. Contact

Privacy questions? support@kempzonline.com.
